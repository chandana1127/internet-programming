About Us

Buy a Cake was founded with the mission of connecting rural cake makers with the rest of the world. We believe that everyone should have access to delicious cakes, no matter where they live.

We work with cake makers in rural India to help them market and sell their cakes online. We also provide them with training and support so that they can produce high-quality cakes that meet the expectations of our customers.

We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible online cake ordering experience. We offer a wide variety of cakes to choose from, and we deliver our cakes fresh to your door.
